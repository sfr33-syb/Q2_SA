FOOD

  • Halo-Halo de Iloko – A famous La Union dessert packed with local ingredients like ube, leche flan, saba, and homemade ice milk. It’s known for being bigger, creamier, and more colorful than the typical halo-halo.
  • Dinengdeng – A classic Ilocano dish made from mixed vegetables simmered in bagoong. It’s simple but full of flavor, often served with grilled fish and rice.
