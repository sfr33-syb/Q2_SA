FESTIVALS

  • Baggak Festival (Bauang) – Mid-December to early January; celebrates the morning star with parades, cultural shows, and pageants.
  • Dinengdeng Festival (Agoo) – May; highlights Ilocano cuisine through cooking contests and food fairs.
  • Sillag Festival of Lights (San Fernando) – First week of April; features light displays and tourism activities at Poro Point.
